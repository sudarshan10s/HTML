How to Become a Billionaire


  1. Start with a brilliant idea that no one has thought of before.

  2. Drop out of college to pursue your dreams (or not, education helps too).

  3. Build a prototype in your garage with duct tape and dreams.

  4. Pitch your idea to investors and convince them it's the next big thing.

  5. Scale your company to millions of users overnight.

  6. Go public and watch your net worth skyrocket.

  7. Retire on a private island and live happily ever after.
